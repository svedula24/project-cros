What We Do.

ProjectCROS takes in donations of computers and other hardware and fundraises money to purchase software and more computers to give to underfunded schools that do not have access to technology or classes on how to use them.

Our Missions

01

Technology for Everyone

Every school should be able to give students access to laptops and other pieces of technology. If they aren't able to, that's where ProjectCROS comes in.

02

Education w/ Iniative

Along with donating laptops and computers to underfunded schools, ProjectCROS's handpicked volunteers will teach students through workshops about a multitude of technology-related topics.

03

Community Building & Engagement

Through numerous volunteer opportunities and short-term jobs for people in Bay Area communities, ProjectCROS unites people to help with one overarching cause.

Teaching Areas

Paving the way to a technologically educated future for all students through workshops.

Adobe Creative Cloud

Including Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, etc.

Web Development

Teaching students programming languages including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP.

Video Editing

Instruction includes how to use different video-editing softwares to creative fun and engaging videos and advertisements.

Programming

Training students on programming languages such as Python, Java, and C++ geared towards competitive programming contests such as the USA Computing Olympiad and Facebook Hacker Cup.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why ProjectCROS?

    ProjectCROS is unique in a sense that it allows major companies and organizations get involved with underfunded and underpriviledged schools by acting as a proprietary medium. Additionally, ProjectCROS gives access for anyone who is willing a chance to volunteer for a cause that has gone overlooked by many.

  • What does a volunteer do?

    Volunteers @ ProjectCROS are repsonsible for helping distribute flyers, advertise campaigns, come in during special events to help with anything such as installing a specific software, help ProjectCROS schools with jobs/events

  • How old do I have to be to volunteer?

    Volunteers in grades 8-12 as well as adults are welcome to help at ProjectCROS.
